About me

2014.08.08

My primary interest is to learn how to design software systems, so as to maximize the productivity of development teams. In doing this I try to look at current technological landscape and investigate software with good design.

History

Grew up in Marosvasarhely, Romania when to Babeş-Bolyai University Faculty of Mathematics and Computer Science. My dissertation was about JavaScript based grid computing. During the time I was an intern at a mobile development company where I did Android development and have worked at an outsource firm where I did test automation. After graduating a moved to Budapest, Hungary where I started to work at a leading financial institution, trough which I had a chance to learn at the international offices in New York and London.
